About Us

The Troup

Hello! We are Buster & Charlie, the Troubadour Twins, and welcome to our whimsical world. Here, tunes tangle with tumbling and melodies merge with mischief! Hailing from the enchanting city of Waterdeep, we invite you on a journey through realms of rhythm and harmony. With Buster's nimble fingers dancing across the strings of his lute and Charlie's enchanting voice weaving tales of wonder, our performances are not just concerts but unforgettable experiences. Drawing inspiration from the bustling streets of Waterdeep, where every corner holds a story and every alley echoes with the promise of adventure, our music is a celebration of life, love, and the joy of discovery. From lively taverns to majestic courts, we've regaled audiences with our melodies, earning a reputation as the troubadours of the fantastical. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer seeking respite from your quests or a weary traveler yearning for tales of far-off lands, we welcome you with open arms and melodies that will transport you to realms beyond imagination. So come and join us as we embark on a musical odyssey filled with laughter, love, and the magic of music.

Members

Buster--Lutist, Lyracist

  • Name: Buster Marx
  • Age: 32
  • Role: Musical Lead

Meet Buster, the masterful lutist and musical maestro behind the enchanting melodies of the Troubadour Twins. Buster's love affair with music began at a young age when he first laid eyes on the intricate curves of a lute. From that moment on, his destiny was set: to serenade the world with his soul-stirring tunes. With fingers as nimble as a rogue's and a heart as vast as the sea, Buster's performances are a testament to his unwavering passion for his craft. Whether he's strumming the strings of his lute with gentle precision or unleashing a flurry of notes in a whirlwind of sound, Buster's music transports listeners to realms of wonder and whimsy. But Buster's talents extend far beyond mere musical mastery. He's a storyteller at heart, weaving tales of heroism, romance, and adventure with each pluck of his strings. His infectious energy and boundless enthusiasm light up every stage he graces, leaving audiences spellbound and craving for more. Offstage, Buster is a jovial soul with a mischievous twinkle in his eye. When he's not captivating audiences with his performances, you can find him exploring the hidden nooks and crannies of Waterdeep, seeking inspiration in every shadowy alley and bustling marketplace. Join Buster on his musical journey and let the enchanting melodies of his lute sweep you away on a magical adventure you'll never forget. The world is our stage, and the music never stops.

Charlie--Vocals, Visuals, Acrobatics

  • Name: Charlie Marx
  • Age: 32
  • Role: Cheif Entertainment Official

Allow me to introduce Charlie, the charismatic vocalist, agile acrobat, and all-around entertainer extraordinaire of the Troubadour Twins. Charlie's passion for performance was ignited at a tender age, fueled by a boundless energy and an insatiable thirst for adventure. With a voice as melodious as a songbird and a stage presence that commands attention, Charlie captivates audiences with his enchanting vocals and dazzling acrobatic feats. Whether he's belting out ballads that tug at the heartstrings or executing gravity-defying flips and spins, Charlie's performances are a mesmerizing blend of music, movement, and magic. But Charlie's talents extend far beyond the confines of the stage. Spinning tales of daring escapades and thrilling exploits with every flick of his wrist and twist of his body, Charlie's infectious enthusiasm and boundless creativity infuse every performance with a sense of wonder and joy, leaving audiences spellbound and eager for more. Offstage, Charlie is a free spirit with a zest for life and a love of exploration. When he's not dazzling crowds with his performances, you can find him roaming the streets of Waterdeep, seeking out new adventures and embracing every opportunity to experience the wonders of the world around him. Join Charlie and let the magic of his music and movement transport you to realms of excitement and delight.

Cluck--Management

  • Name: Cluck
  • Age: 6
  • Role: Head of Operations

Meet Cluck, the feathered manager extraordinaire of the Troubadour Twins! Hatched by the twins in their Waterdeep home, Cluck may not be a great singer, but he more than makes up for it with his sharp business acumen. As the trusted manager of the troupe, Cluck ensures that every aspect of the Troubadour Twins' performances runs like a well-oiled machine. From booking gigs and managing finances to organizing rehearsals and coordinating travel arrangements, Cluck handles it all with poise and professionalism. Despite his lack of hands, Cluck is a master multitasker, effortlessly juggling the myriad responsibilities that come with running a successful musical ensemble. His keen eye for detail and unwavering dedication to the troupe's success make him an indispensable member of the Troubadour Twins family. However, Cluck's talents extend far beyond the realm of management. With a keen ear for music and a natural sense of rhythm, he's been known to lend his vocals to the occasional chorus, adding a touch of whimsy to the troupe's performances. Offstage, Cluck enjoys pecking in the finer patches of dirt around Waterdeep and soaking in the sights and sounds of the city he calls home. When he's not busy managing the troupe, you can often find him perched atop a nearby roof, basking in the warmth of the sun and dreaming up new ways to take the Troubadour Twins to even greater heights.
